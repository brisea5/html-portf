The Best Movies According to Bri

My top 3 movies/series of all-time.

La Reina Del Sur

Very stimulating, a lot of action and drama.It's a Netflix series dealing with the realities of drug cartels.

Bajo La Misma Luna

Very heartwarming sad movie describing the struggles of immigration and the pursuit of the American Dream.

Euphoria

Showcases stuggles of young people in today's society. Themes like addiction,trauma,mental health and sexuality.
